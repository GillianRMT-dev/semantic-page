Sourdough Starter

Sourdough starter is made up of flour, water, and lots of bacteria. You take care of your starter by regularly feeding it fresh water and flour. A healthy sourdough starter with active bacteria will fill with bubbles.

Sourdough Bread

If you mix sourdough starter with water, flour, and salt, let it ferment about eight hours and then bake it in the oven, you will have sourdough bread! However, learning the proper amounts of each ingredient along with a few key techniques can can help you make a not only delicious but also beautiful loaf.

How Different Techniques Affect Your Bread
Technique Texture Crust Notes
High Hydration Open, airy crumb Thin, crispy Harder to shape
Dutch oven baking Soft, chewy Thick, golden Traps steam
Long cold ferment Complex flavor, chewier Darker color Easier scoring
Deep scoring More oven spring Dramatic "ear" Better expansion
